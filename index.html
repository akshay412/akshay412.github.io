About Me

I am Akshay Paralikar, a passionate data scientist pursuing my Master's at New York University. With a robust background in Engineering and three years of experience as a Data Science Consultant, I've honed my skills in Python, SQL, Tableau, and various advanced data analytics tools.
My expertise lies in transforming raw data into actionable insights, developing strategic solutions, and crafting data-driven action plans. I thrive on the daily challenges that working with data presents, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in this ever-evolving field.
When I'm not immersed in data, you might find me tinkering with electronics, creating (or occasionally short-circuiting) innovative solutions to quirky problems.
